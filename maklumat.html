Pernyataan komitmen pelayanan publik UPBU Kelas III Waghete sesuai Undang-Undang Nomor 25 Tahun 2009 tentang Pelayanan Publik.Public service commitment statement of UPBU Class III Waghete pursuant to Law No. 25 of 2009 on Public Services.

MAKLUMAT PELAYANAN
"Dengan ini kami menyatakan sanggup menyelenggarakan pelayanan sesuai dengan standar pelayanan yang telah ditetapkan, dan apabila tidak menepati janji ini, kami siap menerima sanksi sesuai peraturan perundang-undangan yang berlaku." "We hereby declare that we are committed to delivering services in accordance with the established service standards, and should we fail to keep this promise, we are prepared to accept sanctions in accordance with applicable laws and regulations."

Kepala Kantor UPBU Kelas III Waghete
beserta seluruh jajaran pegawaiHead of UPBU Class III Waghete Office
and all staff
