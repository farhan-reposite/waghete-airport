Tugas & FungsiDuties & Functions

Sesuai ketentuan tentang organisasi dan tata kerja Kantor Unit Penyelenggara Bandar Udara di lingkungan Kementerian Perhubungan.In accordance with regulations on the organization and working procedures of Airport Operator Unit Offices within the Ministry of Transportation.

TUGAS / DUTY

UPBU Kelas III Waghete mempunyai tugas melaksanakan pelayanan jasa kebandarudaraan dan jasa terkait bandar udara, serta menjamin keamanan, keselamatan, dan ketertiban penerbangan pada bandar udara yang belum diusahakan secara komersial.UPBU Class III Waghete is tasked with providing airport services and airport-related services, and ensuring aviation security, safety, and order at an airport not yet operated commercially.

FUNGSI / FUNCTIONS

Dalam melaksanakan tugas tersebut, UPBU Waghete menyelenggarakan fungsi:In carrying out these duties, UPBU Waghete performs the following functions:

01

Pelaksanaan penyusunan rencana dan program, evaluasi, dan pelaporan kegiatan bandar udara.Preparation of plans and programs, evaluation, and reporting of airport activities.

02

Pelaksanaan pengoperasian, perawatan, dan perbaikan fasilitas keselamatan, keamanan, sisi udara, sisi darat, serta alat-alat besar bandar udara.Operation, maintenance, and repair of safety and security facilities, airside, landside, and heavy airport equipment.

03

Pelaksanaan pengamanan pelayanan pengangkutan penumpang, awak pesawat udara, bagasi, kargo, dan pos.Security services for the transport of passengers, flight crews, baggage, cargo, and mail.

04

Pelaksanaan pengawasan dan pengendalian keamanan dan ketertiban di lingkungan kerja serta pengoperasian bandar udara.Supervision and control of security and order within the working area and airport operations.

05

Pelaksanaan pelayanan penerbangan dan kemudahan-kemudahan lainnya bagi pengguna jasa bandar udara.Provision of flight services and other conveniences for airport service users.

06

Pelaksanaan pelayanan jasa kebandarudaraan dan jasa terkait bandar udara.Provision of airport services and airport-related services.

07

Pelaksanaan urusan keuangan, kepegawaian, ketatausahaan, kerumahtanggaan, hukum, dan hubungan masyarakat.Administration of finance, personnel, general affairs, household matters, legal affairs, and public relations.

08

Pelaksanaan koordinasi dengan instansi terkait dalam rangka kelancaran operasional bandar udara.Coordination with relevant agencies to ensure smooth airport operations.
