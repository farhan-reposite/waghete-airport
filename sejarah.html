Sejarah Bandar Udara WagheteHistory of Waghete Airport

Dari lapangan terbang tepi Danau Tigi menuju gerbang udara modern Kabupaten Deiyai.From an airstrip on the shores of Lake Tigi to the modern air gateway of Deiyai Regency.

SEBELUM 2014BEFORE 2014

Lapangan Terbang LamaThe Old Airstrip

Waghete dilayani lapangan terbang lama di dekat Danau Tigi. Lokasinya rendah dan sering tergenang air saat danau meluap, sehingga operasional penerbangan kerap terganggu — padahal jalur udara adalah urat nadi transportasi pedalaman Papua Tengah.Waghete was served by an old airstrip near Lake Tigi. Its low-lying location often flooded when the lake overflowed, frequently disrupting flights — even though air routes are the lifeline of transportation in Central Papua's interior.

2011 – 2013

Pembangunan Bandara BaruConstruction of the New Airport

Pemerintah melalui Kementerian Perhubungan membangun Bandara Waghete Baru sebagai bagian dari program pembangunan bandara baru nasional. Lokasi dipilih di dataran yang lebih tinggi dan luas di Distrik Tigi, tetap dekat pusat kota namun aman dari luapan danau.Through the Ministry of Transportation, the government built the New Waghete Airport as part of the national new-airport development program. A higher, more spacious plateau in Tigi District was chosen — still close to town, yet safe from lake flooding.

7 MARET 20147 MARCH 2014

Mulai BeroperasiOperations Begin

Bandara Waghete Baru resmi beroperasi dengan landas pacu 1.400 meter. Penerbangan perdana melayani rute Waghete–Nabire. Masyarakat Deiyai akhirnya memiliki akses udara yang andal sepanjang tahun.The new Waghete Airport officially opened with a 1,400-meter runway. Inaugural flights served the Waghete–Nabire route, finally giving the people of Deiyai reliable year-round air access.

2014 – 2020

Pengembangan FasilitasFacility Development

Runway diperlebar dan fasilitas sisi darat maupun sisi udara terus ditingkatkan: terminal penumpang, terminal kargo, gedung PKP-PK, powerhouse, hingga fasilitas keamanan penerbangan.The runway was widened and both landside and airside facilities were continuously improved: passenger terminal, cargo terminal, ARFF building, powerhouse, and aviation security facilities.

SAAT INITODAY

UPBU Kelas III WagheteUPBU Class III Waghete

Sebagai Unit Penyelenggara Bandar Udara Kelas III di Provinsi Papua Tengah, Bandara Waghete menjadi satu-satunya jalur transportasi udara reguler dari dan menuju Kabupaten Deiyai, melayani penerbangan perintis penumpang dan kargo ke Nabire, Timika, dan sekitarnya.As a Class III Airport Operator Unit in Central Papua Province, Waghete Airport is the only regular air link to and from Deiyai Regency, serving pioneer passenger and cargo flights to Nabire, Timika, and beyond.
